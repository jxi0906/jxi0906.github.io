Courier Express


Courier Express is a .NET application created to track the customer's package. Users input customer's tracking number, current location and destination. Also, users need to choose if the customer requests express shipment, select a carrier and fill shipping date and EST of delivery into the form. When all the requested information are put into the form, users can click "Insert" button to record shipment to database. If users want to update customer's information, they can select the shipment, and then change the information and click "Update Record" button. Or they may cancel this operation if they do not want to change anything on the shipment. Users also can delete the shipment based on successfully shipped. This application also contains the proper validation on tracking number, current location, destination, shipping date and EST. To be specifically, the tracking number must be 8 digit number. The current location and destination must be lower than or equal to 25 characters, and they cannot be numbers and some special symbols. The shipping date and EST cannot be earlier than today's date, and EST must be later than shipping date.
